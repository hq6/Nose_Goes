Have a job you don’t want to do? Use Nose Goes! Press Start to begin a simple nose goes, or press the Settings button to customize the message, color, and type for your nose. When you are happy with your nose, press start to begin. Once you start, your friends will get a notification for them to press the nose, and their reaction times will be recorded in a table. The last person to press the nose will lose, and your group of friends will be notified of the results. Good luck!
